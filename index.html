Features
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.
One app replaces your notepad, spreadsheet, calendar, and invoicing software.

Estimates & Invoices

Branded PDF estimates with your logo, line items, and taxes. Convert to invoices in one tap. QR codes, custom templates, and payment terms.

Accept Payments

Stripe online payments, Tap to Pay on iPhone, cash, check, Venmo, Zelle. Send payment links. Track partial payments and automatic reminders.

Scheduling & Calendar

Schedule jobs with dates, times, and priority. Auto-sync to Apple or Google Calendar. Recurring jobs weekly, monthly, or quarterly.

Time Tracking

Clock in and out per job with GPS. Track breaks. Daily, weekly, and monthly summaries. Know exactly where your hours go.

Team Management

Invite workers, assign jobs, control permissions. Team members see assigned work, accept or decline, and update status from their phone.

Client Management

Contacts, addresses, notes. Job history and revenue per client. One-tap call, text, or navigate to any client location.

Reports & Tax Summaries

Revenue, profit & loss, tax summaries, conversion rates. Export PDF or CSV. Top services, average job value, and trends.

Expense Tracking

Materials, fuel, tools, business expenses. Attach receipt photos. Link expenses to jobs. Profit margins and annual deductions at a glance.

Photos & Documentation

Before, during, and after photos with GPS. Document scanning with OCR. A visual record of every job you complete.
