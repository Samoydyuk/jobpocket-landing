Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an estimate?

Open a job, tap "Create Estimate", add line items with descriptions and prices, then tap "Generate PDF" to create a professional estimate.

How do I sync with my calendar?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Calendar Sync, enable the feature, and select which calendar to sync your jobs to. All scheduled jobs will automatically appear in your calendar.

Can I customize my invoice design?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Document Design to add your company logo, customize colors, and set up your business information that appears on estimates and invoices.

How do I track payments?

When viewing a job with an invoice, tap "Record Payment" to mark partial or full payments. The app tracks outstanding balances automatically.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your information is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at support@jobpocket.app to request account deletion. We will process your request and remove all your data from our servers.
